Pancake recipe

Description

perfect recipe for classic pancakes, just like your parents use to make

Ingredients

  • 250g plain flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 250 ml buttermilk

Directions

  1. Sift the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Make a hole in the middle and add the egg.
  3. Mix them quickly after breaking the yolk and pouring in the buttermilk until they become stick batter .
  4. Do not beat when mixing as gluten will appears in the flour, which will prevent the pancakes from rising.
  5. Fry the mixture in hot griddle pan and served when it is still hot
